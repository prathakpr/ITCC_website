About Into The Coaching Class

ITCC: Into The Coaching Class is a horror-action 3D game filled with suspense and thrilling twists.

Into The Coaching Class" is a horror game that takes place in a fictional coaching class. The game is filled with creepy events and metaphors that the player must solve in order to progress. The game is available on both PC and mobile devices and will soon be available on VR devices as well. The player takes on the role of a student in the class who must uncover the dark secrets of the school while navigating through a variety of terrifying obstacles and challenges. Will you be able to solve the mysteries of the Creepy Coaching Class and make it out alive?
